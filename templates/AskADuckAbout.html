What we do:

Our plan is to help those who suffer from difficulties of vague coverages from insurances. The tedium and difficulty of knowing how much one has to pay for prescriptions is time consuming, and our goal is to streamline this process as much as possible. Utilizing AI advanced search features, we filter the information to only the essentials directly pulled from the insurance companies, thus minimizing innacurate results from AI and removing the hassle of overly specific AI prompts or long google searches.
